About the dataset

General description: 
Diabetes is a complex metabolic disorder that poses significant health risks if not managed effectively. Its prevalence worldwide has led to a growing need for accurate and early diagnostic methods

Donor: 
Vikas Ukani

Source: 
The dataset was originally sourced from the National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vikasukani/diabetes-data-set/data) 


Attributes:

    Pregnancies
    Plasma glucose concentration
    Blood pressure (mm Hg)
    Triceps skin fold thickness (mm)
    Serum insulin (mu U/ml)
    Body mass index (weight in kg/(height in m)^2)
    Diabetes pedigree function
    Age (years)
    Outcome variable (0 or 1)
